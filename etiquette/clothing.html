Christian clothing

Dress simply and modestly; clothing should support prayer, not distract from it.

At a glance

  • Avoid loud styles; choose clean, modest clothing suitable for bowing.
  • Women: a simple head covering is often customary; avoid transparent or tight clothing.
  • Men: neat shoes and trousers; remove caps; avoid sportswear and sleeveless tops.
  • Comfort + modesty help you focus in prayer.
