How to light candles (and prayers)

A candle is a small offering and a prayer before the Lord or a saint.

At a glance

  • Light the candle carefully and place it safely before the appropriate icon.
  • Simple prayers: “Lord Jesus Christ, Son of God, have mercy on me,” “Most Holy Theotokos, save us,” or “Saint N., pray to God for us.”
  • On major feasts, you may hold candles during parts of the service—follow the parish custom.
  • If unsure where to place a candle, ask an attendant.
