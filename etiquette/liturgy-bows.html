Bows at the Holy Liturgy

During the Liturgy, bowing is deliberate and simple—especially at greetings and blessings.

At a glance

  • At “Peace to all,” make a quiet waist bow (often without the sign of the Cross).
  • During readings and key prayers, keep movements gentle; avoid walking around.
  • At the dismissal/cross-veneration, bow respectfully; if you communed, you usually do not kiss the priest’s hand.
  • When in doubt, follow what the priest and regular faithful do.
Local practice may vary—ask the priest or deacon.
